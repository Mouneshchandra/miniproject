Welcome to EcoFree

EcoFree - Recycling Waste Responsibly

  • EcoFree is a platform dedicated to efficient recycling and waste management.
  • Our mission is to create a sustainable future through responsible waste disposal and recycling practices.

Our Goals:

  • Simplify waste segregation and recycling for individuals, households, and businesses.
  • Accept various waste materials such as plastic bottles, electronic gadgets, paper, metal, and glass.
  • Transform waste into reusable products.

What We Do:

  • Segregation Assistance: Guide users in identifying and sorting recyclable materials.
  • Waste Collection: Provide convenient options for disposing of recyclable waste.
  • Recycling Transformation: Convert collected materials into sustainable products like recycled plastic, metal, and paper items.

Materials We Accept:

  • Plastics: Including bottles, containers, and packaging materials.
  • Electronics: Gadgets and e-waste for responsible recycling.
  • Paper: Used paper, cardboard, and office waste.
  • Glass: Bottles, jars, and glassware.
  • Metals: Scrap metal, aluminum cans, and other recyclable metals.

Our Vision:

  • Empower communities with eco-friendly waste management solutions.
  • Reduce dependency on landfills.
  • Contribute to a cleaner and greener environment.

Through our user-friendly platform, supported by recycling tips, resources, and accessible contact channels, EcoFree is your trusted partner in protecting the planet, one recyclable item at a time. Join us in making a tangible difference!

Facts and FAQs

Did You Know?

  • Recycling one ton of paper saves 17 trees.
  • Glass can be recycled endlessly without losing quality.
  • Electronic waste is the fastest-growing waste stream in the world.

What materials can I recycle?

We accept plastics, electronics, paper, glass, and metals.

How do I book a collection?

You can book a collection via our booking section or contact us directly.

Is there a fee for waste pickup?

No, our pickup service is free for recyclable materials.
